Production Capacity

Target: 50,000 units per year

Achieved: 52,000 units per year (104% of target)

Required printers: 4 x UltiMaker S5 running 2 shifts

Cycle time achieved: 138 seconds per unit (target: 144 seconds)

Lessons Learned & Recommendations

  • Process stability: FDM with PETG provides consistent results after proper calibration
  • Supplier variability: Regular communication and shared metrology data improves interchangeability
  • Assembly integration: Early testing on FESTO line identified tolerance issues before production
  • Scaling recommendation: Add 1 backup printer to maintain 50k capacity during maintenance

Conclusion

The Phones-R-Us project was completed successfully within the 9-week timeline. All casings meet dimensional requirements, pass FESTO CP Lab assembly validation, and are fully interchangeable with existing supplier components. Production capacity of 50,000 units per annum is achievable with the recommended printer configuration.

Design Process Overview

The Phones-R-Us project required two casings: the bottom casing (drawing provided by client) and the top casing (reverse engineered from an existing part). Both models were created in Autodesk Fusion 360 and exported as STL files for additive manufacturing.

Reverse Engineering Process (Top Casing)

  • Physical part received from Phones-R-Us
  • Measured using digital callipers and CMM
  • CAD model created in Fusion 360
  • Iterations adjusted to match drawing tolerances
  • Final STL exported for 3D printing

From Drawing to CAD (Bottom Casing)

  • Client drawing with critical dimensions provided
  • Fusion 360 sketch based on drawing dimensions
  • 3D extrusion and feature creation
  • Dimensional verification against drawing
  • STL export with optimal mesh resolution

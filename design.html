The Phones-R-Us project involved the design and development of two precision-engineered phone casings, each requiring a different modelling approach. The bottom casing was created directly from a fully defined client drawing, while the top casing required reverse engineering from a physical component.

Autodesk Fusion 360 was used to produce accurate 2D drawings and detailed 3D CAD models. Where drawing data was incomplete, engineering judgement and cross-referencing between views were applied to ensure dimensional accuracy and consistency.

Final models were exported as STL files, ensuring readiness for additive manufacturing and maintaining high geometric fidelity throughout the workflow.

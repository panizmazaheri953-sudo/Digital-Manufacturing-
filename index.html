About the project

Our Objective

As a specialist manufacturer of additively produced components, we deliver high-quality top and bottom phone casings that meet strict dimensional requirements and integrate seamlessly with components supplied by other vendors — supporting up to 50,000 units per year.

Our Approach

FDM-based additive manufacturing was selected for its flexibility and rapid production capabilities. This portfolio documents our process evolution — from initial trials through to final production — focused on achieving a stable and scalable solution.

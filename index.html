Welcome to Paniz Manufacturing

As a specialist manufacturer of additively produced components, our objective is to deliver high-quality top and bottom phone casings that meet strict dimensional requirements and integrate seamlessly with components supplied by other vendors. We aim to support production volumes of up to 50,000 units per year while maintaining consistency and reliability.

To achieve this, FDM-based additive manufacturing has been selected due to its flexibility and rapid production capabilities. However, producing components suitable for assembly-line integration requires careful process development, validation, and optimisation.

This blog documents the evolution of our manufacturing process, from initial trials through to final production, with a focus on achieving a stable and scalable solution.

Current Project: Phones-R-Us

Target: 50,000 interchangeable phone casings per year

Timeline: 9-week prototype delivery
